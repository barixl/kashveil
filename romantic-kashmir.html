Package Overview

Discover the breathtaking landscapes and serene beauty of Kashmir with our specially curated Romantic Kashmir Escape. This 5-day journey is designed exclusively for couples looking for a mix of leisure, luxury, and romance.

From a moonlit stay on a premium houseboat in Dal Lake to the snow-capped peaks of Gulmarg and the lush valleys of Pahalgam, every moment is crafted to give you memories that will last a lifetime. Enjoy private transfers, romantic candlelight dinners, and expert local guidance.

Day-wise Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Srinagar & Houseboat Stay

Srinagar

Arrive at Srinagar Airport where our representative will greet you. Transfer to a luxury houseboat on Dal Lake. In the late afternoon, enjoy a romantic Shikara ride on the lake, witnessing the vibrant floating markets and the breathtaking sunset. Evening includes a special welcome dinner.

Shikara Ride Welcome Dinner

Day 2: Srinagar Local Sightseeing

Srinagar

After breakfast, check out from the houseboat and check into your premium hotel. Spend the day exploring the famous Mughal Gardens - Nishat Bagh, Shalimar Bagh, and Chashma Shahi. In the evening, visit the Shankaracharya Temple for a panoramic view of the city.

Day 3: Day Trip to Gulmarg

Gulmarg

Drive to Gulmarg, the "Meadow of Flowers". Enjoy the scenic drive and the world-famous Gondola ride (Phase 1 & 2) to Apharwat Peak. Spend time in the snow, take photographs, and enjoy the crisp mountain air. Return to Srinagar by evening.

Day 4: Day Trip to Pahalgam

Pahalgam

Proceed for a day excursion to Pahalgam, the "Valley of Shepherds". Visit the saffron fields of Pampore and the ruins of Awantipora on the way. In Pahalgam, enjoy the serene Lidder River and optionally visit Aru Valley or Betaab Valley. Return to Srinagar for your final night.

Day 5: Departure

Srinagar

After a hearty breakfast, pack your beautiful memories. Our driver will drop you at Srinagar Airport for your onward journey, bidding you a warm farewell.
