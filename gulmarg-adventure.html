Package Overview

Embrace the winter wonderland of Gulmarg, the ultimate destination for snow lovers and adventure enthusiasts. This 4-day action-packed itinerary focuses on experiencing the best winter sports in the Himalayas.

Day-wise Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival & Transfer to Gulmarg

Gulmarg

Arrive at Srinagar airport and transfer directly to your premium ski resort in Gulmarg. Spend the evening acclimatizing to the winter weather and enjoy a cozy dinner.

Day 2: Gondola Ride & Snow Activities

Gulmarg

Experience the famous Gulmarg Gondola up to Phase 2 (Apharwat Peak). Enjoy breathtaking views and engage in snow activities like sledding or a snowmobile ride.

Day 3: Skiing Lessons

Gulmarg

Spend the day learning to ski on the gentle slopes of Gulmarg with a professional instructor. Equipment and guidance will be provided.

Day 4: Departure

Srinagar

Transfer back to Srinagar airport for your onward journey, carrying the thrill of the adventure back home.
