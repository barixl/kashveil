Ladakh Bike Expedition

The ultimate adventure through the land of high passes, featuring Leh, Nubra Valley, and Pangong Lake.

Package Overview

Ride through the world's highest motorable passes and witness the raw beauty of the Himalayas. This expedition is designed for thrill-seekers who want to experience Ladakh on two wheels.

Day-wise Itinerary

Day 1: Leh Arrival & Acclimatization

Leh

Arrive at Leh and rest for the day to acclimatize to the high altitude.

Day 2: Local Leh Sightseeing

Leh

Visit Shanti Stupa, Leh Palace, and the local market.

Day 3: Leh to Nubra Valley via Khardung La

Nubra Valley

Cross the famous Khardung La pass and reach the cold desert of Nubra.

Day 4: Nubra Valley to Pangong Lake

Pangong Lake

Ride along the Shyok river to reach the mesmerizing Pangong Lake.

Day 5: Return to Leh

Leh

Ride back to Leh via Chang La pass.

Day 6: Magnetic Hill & Sangam

Leh

Explore the outskirts of Leh, including the Magnetic Hill and Indus-Zanskar confluence.

Day 7: Leisure Day

Leh

A free day to explore Leh on your own or relax.

Day 8: Departure

Leh

Transfer to Leh airport.
